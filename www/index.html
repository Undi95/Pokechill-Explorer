🗼 Battle Tower (Spiraling Tower)

Infinitely scaling challenge where each defeated Pokémon increases difficulty. Type immunities become resistances. Your highest floor resets when league rotation changes.

📋 Rules

  • Start from floor 1 each entry
  • 1 reward per NEW floor reached
  • Max 50 item rewards (floors 2-51)
  • +2 Golden Bottle Caps per floor
  • Max 100 Golden Bottle Caps total
  • Ribbon at floor 30 for all team members
  • Type rotation changes every 3 days

🏭 Battle Factory

Test your skills with rental Pokémon! Build the best team possible and aim for the highest score. Rental Pokémon are random every 3 days.

📋 Rules

  • Use rental Pokémon only
  • Score points by winning battles
  • Rewards every 200 points after 1000
  • Max 30 item rewards
  • +1 Golden Bottle Cap per reward (max 100)
  • Same reward pool as Battle Tower

🎯 Scoring

Minimum score of 1000 required for rewards.

Next reward at:
1000 + (200 × rewards earned)

Example: 1st reward at 1200, 2nd at 1400...

🗼 Battle Tower Rotations

Each rotation features a random type. Pokémon are randomly selected from that type based on your current division (C, B, A, S). Plan your team accordingly!

ℹ️ How it works:
The type changes randomly every 3 days. For each floor, a Pokémon is randomly chosen from the current type pool matching your division. Higher floors = higher division Pokémon!

⚔️ Battle Arena (VS Frontier)

Face 5 powerful trainers in succession! Each trainer has 6 Pokémon with randomized moves. Daily reset brings new trainers and new challenges.

📋 How It Works

Défi Quotidien: Toutes les 12 heures, 5 dresseurs aléatoires sont sélectionnés parmi un pool de 18. Vous devez vaincre les 5 d'affilée!

Équipes Aléatoires: Les 6 Pokémon de chaque dresseur sont sélectionnés aléatoirement dans la division de leur ligue. Cliquez sur un dresseur pour voir les Pokémon possibles!

Attaques Aléatoires: Chaque Pokémon connaît 4 attaques complètement aléatoires. On ne sait jamais à quoi s'attendre!

Effets de Terrain: Chaque combat a 1-3 effets de terrain aléatoires qui changent toutes les 12 heures. Adaptez votre stratégie!

🏭 Battle Factory Target Pokémon

In Battle Factory, you face a single passive target Pokémon that only knows Splash (it doesn't attack). The target is randomly selected from the current rotation's division. No double weaknesses allowed!

ℹ️ How it works:
The target Pokémon changes with each rotation. It's selected randomly from the current division and knows ONLY Splash. The game specifically excludes Pokémon with double weaknesses to any type!
